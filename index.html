01 — Who We Are
SPMEst. Waterford

A boutique football consultancy built around the player.

Southeast Player Management supports young football players (aged 14+) and their families across Southeast Ireland with personalised, high-level guidance and development support.

We work with a small number of players at any one time. Our focus is long-term success — on the pitch and off it — and our role is independent: every decision is made in the best interests of the player.

02 — Our Approach

We act as independent advisors — focused solely on the player.

Clubs and agents play important roles, but their perspectives are shaped by their own structures, timelines, and commercial interests. Players and families don't always have access to fully independent, long-term guidance.

Southeast Player Management exists to provide that clarity.
